Art Moderne Interior Design Style

Art Moderne originated from the United States in the early 1930s and trended until the late 1940s. Those days, this interior design style was all about bigger, bolder, and brassier.


Minimalist Interior Design Style

This minimalist interior design style originated in Australia and is still very popular there. The style emphasizes minimalism in all aspects of interior design.


Scandinavian Interior Design Style

The Scandinavian interior design style is a combination of various design styles that evolved in the 20th century in the five
Nordic countries of Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, and Iceland. The Scandinavian style is about a minimalist interior, utility, and simplicity.

The Shabby Chic Style is characterized by laid-back vibes, airy lighting fixtures, antique-touch furniture, and a vintage-inspired charm.
